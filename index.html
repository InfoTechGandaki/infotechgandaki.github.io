About


Infotech Club aims to engage students in Information Technology in a fun and meaningful way and helping them to learn skills such as use of digital media, arduino technologies, cyber security, programming, coding and games development.

Through different wrokshops, tech-talks & competitions , students will better appreciate technology and learn about web-app development, game development, robotics and many more.
